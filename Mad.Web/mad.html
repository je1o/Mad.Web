ABOUT


I am a Sydney based photographer specialising in portraiture, headshots, and events. I am also a graphic designer with an advertising background with over 10 years of design experience. I’ve worked with various industries handling local and international brands.


As a graphic designer, I use design to attract people to what I have created. From posters to adverts, I have used design to draw people in and take a closer look. As a photographer, I use photographs to express emotion and connect the viewers to the image. This website is my continuous pursuit to connect humans and emotions with photos and design.
