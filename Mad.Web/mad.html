PROFILE

I am a graphic designer currently based in Sydney, Australia. Backed up with an advertising background and 10+ years of design experience, I have created designs for most print media. I have gained experience from working with two event management companies in the Philippines, a design studio based in Singapore, and one of the top financial banks in the world.


Having designed predominantly for printed media I enjoy playing with typography and finding new ways to create stunning designs. I have refined my skills and creative process in designing for posters, flyers, corporate branding, annual reports, and other printed materials.


I am also into photography, which helps me in my creative process in designing prints like annual reports, brochures, property advertisements and other marketing materials that requires imagery to enhance the design.
